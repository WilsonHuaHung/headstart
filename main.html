PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The HeadStart Fellowship is a free student-led mentorship program designed to give college freshmen a head start on their professional and personal development. The program will give students exposure to professional opportunities within the Technology, Finance, and Consulting fields.



PROGRAM DETAILS

  • One year program for college freshmen
  • Small cohort with 1:1 mentorship and weekly meetings
  • General education curriculum on subjects ranging from financial literacy to getting involved on campus
  • Detailed professional sessions on Technology, Finance & Consulting
    • Community of like-minded ambitious individuals


MISSION

We've all had amazing mentors that have helped us get to where we are today. Now that we are in the position to do the same, we want to give back and be those mentors for the next generation.
